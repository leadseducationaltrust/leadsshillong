About Our School

Affiliated with MBOSE

Mrs. Barrilyne Lyngdoh

Principal, LEADS School

"Leadership is the capacity to translate vision into reality."

Welcome to LEADS Higher Secondary School. As the Principal, it is my privilege to lead an institution that is deeply committed to the holistic growth of our students in Shillong.

Our curriculum, strictly aligned with MBOSE standards, is designed to challenge the intellect while our extracurricular activities nurture the heart. We believe that education at LEADS is not just about academic scores; it is about building a moral compass that guides our students to become the leaders of tomorrow.

I invite you to explore our campus at Langkerding and witness the dedication of our faculty and the vibrancy of our student community.

Warm Regards,
Mrs. Barrilyne Lyngdoh
